How it works

Up and running
in 60 seconds.

No complicated setup. No syncing. Just open the app and start showing up.

1

Add your habits

Pick what you want to track — daily, weekly, monthly, or one-off. Set it once and Daily takes care of the rest.

2

Check in every day

Open the Today tab, tick off what you've done. One tap per habit. Your streak grows automatically.

3

Watch it add up

Streaks, calendar views, goal rings, and progress charts — see exactly how consistent you've been.
